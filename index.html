SHAKES & DRINKS

  • BANANA SHAKE

    High CALORIE ₹49/-(300ml) ₹79/-(500ml)

    A basic banana shake requires ripe bananas, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. Additional ingredients like ice cubes or nuts can be added for variation.

  • BANANA+CHEEKU SHAKE

    High CALORIE ₹49/-(300ml) ₹79/-(500ml)

    For a banana and cheeku shake, combine ripe bananas, cheeku pulp, milk, and a touch of sugar or honey in a blender. Blend until smooth, and enjoy the creamy, fruity goodness!

  • BADAM RAGDA DRINK

    SPECIAL ₹69/-(300ml) ₹109/-(500ml)

    For a badam (almond) shake,blend them with milk, sugar, and a pinch of cardamom. Serve chilled for a refreshing and nutritious treat.

  • MUNAKKA DRINK

    SPECIAL ₹69/-(300ml) ₹109/-(500ml)

    Soak munakkas overnight, blend with water, honey, and lemon juice, then strain for a refreshing drink..

  • KEEWI SMOOTHIE

    WEIGHT LOSS ₹59/-(250ml) ₹69/-(300ml)

    For a kiwi smoothie, blend ripe kiwi fruit with yogurt, a splash of orange juice, and a drizzle of honey until smooth. Enjoy the tropical and tangy flavors!.

  • STRAWBERRY SMOOTHIE

    WEIGHT LOSS ₹59/-(250ml) ₹69/-(300ml)

    For a strawberry smoothie, blend fresh strawberries with yogurt, a touch of milk, and a teaspoon of honey until creamy and smooth. Indulge in the delightful sweetness of strawberries!

  • MOSAMBI JUICE

    CITRUS JUICES ₹39/-(300ml) ₹59/-(500ml)

    For a Mosambi (sweet lime) juice, extract the juice from fresh Mosambi fruits, mix with a pinch of salt and sugar, and dilute with water as desired. Serve chilled for a refreshing citrus drink.

  • TANGERINE/KEENU JUICE

    CITRUS JUICES ₹29/-(300ml) ₹49/-(500ml)

    For tangerine juice, squeeze fresh tangerines and enjoy the tangy, sweet flavor. Optionally, chill the juice or mix with a splash of sparkling water for a fizzy twist.
